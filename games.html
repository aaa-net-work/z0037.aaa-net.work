ROAMANCE

ROAMance (PC-VR 2024, UnrealEngine UE427 c++ and BP, Production: Revolver and Medea, Directors: Stan Liguzinski, Ibrahim Quraishi), is a unconventional dating encounter in VR. Multiplayer via Steam API for 2 persons, a get-to-know-each-other in a playful & arty environment. In this project i was the leading gameplay programmer and at some point i became a part of the conception, as various scene and gameplay ideas by me where added. Roamance is supposed to have its premiere at art festivals in 2024, a work in progress is shown at Amaze Berlin 2024 .

Beat detection 2023

I worked with various beat-detection methods in Unreal4.27 - like Synesthesia Plugin and others to trigger and shape movements of interactive objects in time. The difficulty i found is, that audio-frequency-by-amplitude discrimination works nicely but leaves us without a release (hold) function, as well needs a further normalization of all frequencies to allow detection for dynamical changes in loudness of the music. Then, the movement triggered by beat detection basis on parametrized changes in direction, color, size, velocity and rotation within a tick-like function. The interactive objects in their interactivity (grabbable, destroyable) and in their movement where developed for ROAMANCE project to replicate for multiplayer.

Bubble Room VR 2023

Bubble Room (UnrealEngine 4.27, c++ and BP, VR for PC-VR or APK), may become a game at some future time with a geometric and axiomatic basis in look and haptics. Until here it is just a "spline drawing on meshes" and "using mathematical functions for instanced objects in 3D" working example. This test involved how much nausea a missing floor for VR players is causing.

Complex Rowing // Paddling Mechanics 2023

VR mechanics with grabbed paddle on a boat. The methods reads out whether you paddle forward or backward, left or right side. In addition it scales velocity and boat rotation with rotation of paddle to water surface and angle of paddle to boat axis. Developed for ROAMANCE - most first-VR users was so too complex and was used in a much more simplified version.

Full c++ VR Pawn 2023

Based on lectures of Nick Rewkowski, i developed this fully c++ based VR Pawn in UE427 (and UE52). Controllable via motion controller or with mouse and keyboard, the focus lied on variations of grab mechanics without using the engines embedded functionality for that.

Visualisation of Architecture in VR 2020-2022

In 2020 i started as product owner and programmer to realize architectual visualtions in a VR game for UnrealEstate GmBH Augsburg . The used 3D Models originated from real estate concepts and viewing these buildings inside and outside in VR enabled a close to reality impression for these projects. Light settings, weather change and environmental surroundings tried to come close as possible to reality. Performance optimization and intuitive interaction (in detail: multiple functionality on one controller button, do not use to many buttons!) was the challenge to solve in this project. The video shows an in VR gameplay in our showroom project.
