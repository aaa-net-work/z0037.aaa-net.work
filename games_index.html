BuzzyBumbleBee Adventure

BuzzyBumbleBee Adventure Game Conception

(Game Conception, Production: UnrealEstate GmbH 2024, Conception and Art Direction: me and Jacob Brosda), is violence free flight simulator adventure, where the player plays a bumble bee flying from one blossom to another, collecting honey and pollen for food to fly and to store in the bumble bee home. The game is a flight simulator in the scale of a bumble bee flying in an ocean of giant plants and flowers. This game is about the micro cosmos and the physical interaction. Flying the bumble bee is affected by gravity, managing the food and energy reserves to reach the next blossom as improving your abilities with a short and longterm perk system. Visualisation of Architecture in VR 2020-2022

In 2020 i started as product owner and programmer to develop architectual visualisations in a VR game for UnrealEstate GmBH Augsburg for the purpose of high level PR material for real estate projects. Interactive concepts the suit into this context, like changing floor materials, light or wheather settings rounded the fully immersive VR experience. . The used 3D Models originated from real construction plans. Viewing these buildings inside and outside in VR enabled a close to reality impression for these projects. Light settings, weather change and environmental surroundings tried to come close as possible to reality. Performance optimization and intuitive interaction was the challenge to solve in this project. The video shows an in VR gameplay in our showroom project.
