Found Footage Animation of a chronology of Berlin's student strike in 2003/2004 against study fees. Experimental documentary that presents uncommented official newspapers and student protest material, as well as documentary sound and video material of various actions. The documementary aims for a reconstruction of the situation by a strict commitment to the chronology. It presents deep insides in the interplay between public media and protest movements, next to document the evolution and decay of the movement and the reaction of berlins and Germanys politics to it itself.
